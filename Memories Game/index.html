An interactive story about what remains

Some debts are paid with time. Yours can be paid with memory.

Rent is due in three days. The city has built a market for forgetting, and desperate people line up to sell pieces of their lives. Tonight you decide how much of yourself can be traded before the person left behind stops feeling like you.

What you will trade

What changes

Dialogue, available choices, your sense of self, and how other people remember you.

How it ends

There are multiple endings. Some leave you poorer and whole. Some leave you rich and gone.
